NFT Marketplace / Usecase

The Gojo token will work as a governance token, holders will have the ability to vote on the NFTs that will be listed on the marketplace as official Gojo Satoru fan NFTs. After the voting process, the artist will pay a fee which will be distributed to the token holders. The distribution of the fees will be relative to the amount of Gojo tokens held by each user.

More details on how it functions will be released in the Whitepaper. The roadmap below details a general idea on how the future is planned.
